Registration

The workshop will happen in a hybrid mode, allowing both online and in person participation. In both cases, the registration is mandatory.

  • For those who plan to come to Paris for the workshop (organizers' prefered choice), please register using the ICALP website
  • If you cannot make it to Paris, please fill this form. Online participation is free but you have to register (you will receive a link to participate to the workshop in the days before the event).
